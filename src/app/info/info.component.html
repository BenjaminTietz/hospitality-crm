Hospitality CRM

This is a simple CRM for hospitality businesses. It is built with Angular and Firebase.

Features:

  • 1. Create an account for your booking agents.
  • 2. Set up your properties.
  • 3. Add your bookings.
  • 4. Edit or cancel your bookings.
  • 5. View & change details of your guests.
